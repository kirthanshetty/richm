Company Information (headline paragraph)

subheadline paragraph

This is paragraph text - Richemont International S.A. (hereinafter referred to as "RICHEMONT"), having its registered office at 50, chemin de la Chênaie, CH - 1293 Bellevue/Geneva respectively the data controller, the Human Resources department, the processor and screening body responsible for the processing of Personal Information which is the object of the present Privacy Statement with regard to job applications, shall process Personal Information submitted to RICHEMONT both in paper and electronic form. Applicants may voluntarily submit or be asked to provide their résumé, curriculum vitae or other personal information such as name, email address, postal address, telephone number and other information regarding past employment (hereinafter referred to as "Personal Information"). The Personal Information provided by applicants or by authorised agents acting on their behalf is obligatory.

Paragraph with link

Should applicants have any queries about the use of their Personal Information, they should contact RICHEMONT at: group.humanresources@richemont.com or write to RICHEMONT International SA, C/O Human Resources, 50, chemin de la Chênaie, CH - 1293 Bellevue/Geneva, Switzerland.

Red normal - By clicking on the “consent” button in the e-recruitment application the candidate confirms that he/she has read and given their explicit consent to the processing of their Personal Information and to the disclosure of their Personal Information as described above.

Blue normal - By clicking on the “consent” button in the e-recruitment application the candidate confirms that he/she has read and given their explicit consent to the processing of their Personal Information and to the disclosure of their Personal Information as described above.
