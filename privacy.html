INFORMATION AND CONSENT REGARDING THE PROCESSING OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

APPLICANTS ARE STRONGLY ADVISED TO READ THIS STATEMENT CAREFULLY BEFORE SUBMITTING INFORMATION ABOUT THEMSELVES OR THEIR CV.

Richemont International S.A. (hereinafter referred to as "RICHEMONT"), having its registered office at 50, chemin de la Chenaie, CH - 1293 Bellevue/Geneva respectively the data controller, the Human Resources department, the processor and screening body responsible for the processing of Personal Information which is the object of the present Privacy Statement with regard to job applications, shall process Personal Information submitted to RICHEMONT both in paper and electronic form. Applicants may voluntarily submit or be asked to provide their resume, curriculum vitae or other personal information such as name, email address, postal address, telephone number and other information regarding past employment (hereinafter referred to as "Personal Information"). The Personal Information provided by applicants or by authorised agents acting on their behalf is obligatory.

In the event that consent is withheld or Personal Information not provided, RICHEMONT may reject applications, such Personal Information being required to evaluate candidates during the assessment and recruitment process as well as to contact and correspond with them to respond to such applications. Furthermore, should applicants be employed by RICHEMONT, all Personal Information will be incorporated into a Personal Information file and may be used for the purposes of employee administration (including payrolls, pensions, work management and other employee-related matters).

Personal Information is shared within RICHEMONT departments' and the group Maisons' for operational reasons (for example, to evaluate applicants' suitability). Personal Information may also be disclosed to external agencies where RICHEMONT has a legal obligation to inform such agencies and, in the event of employment, to tax departments, loan companies, local authorities, etc.

Furthermore, applicant and/or employee Personal Information may be transmitted to affiliated companies both in Switzerland and abroad, notably those companies within the Richemont Group of Luxury companies which is present in many parts of the world.

With respect to the processing of Personal Information carried out by RICHEMONT, applicants and employees maintain the rights granted to Data Subjects in accordance with the terms of the Swiss Data Protection law (RS 235.1 Loi federale sur la protection des donnees (LPD) dated 19th June 1992) and modifications thereto, such as, in particular, the right to receive confirmation as to the existence or non-existence of such Personal Information, the manner in which such Personal Information is used, the right to obtain the communication, in an understandable form, of the processed Personal Information and of their origin. Applicants may also request that Personal Information is removed should they not be employed or are no longer employed by RICHEMONT, updated or otherwise modified or that such Personal Information be made anonymous.

If applicants do not wish RICHEMONT to use their Personal Information for such purposes, they should not submit their application to RICHEMONT.

RICHEMONT will use its best endeavours to ensure that Personal Information is kept secure, in particular, that access to such Personal Information will be secured and password protected at all times, the access profile being made available to authorised RICHEMONT personnel only.

Our employees are part of a global family working in a fair, open and trusted environment. It is important for us to ensure that new employees joining the Group have a strong commitment to our shared values.

We therefore reserve the right to ask all candidates in the final stages of recruitment to undergo a pre-employment screening. Our approach to pre-employment screening is based on the principles of transparency, mutual respect and is conducted in line with all applicable regulations.

Should applicants have any queries about the use of their Personal Information, they should contact RICHEMONT at: group.humanresources©richemont.com or write to RICHEMONT International SA, C/O Human Resources, 50, chemin de la Chenaie, CH - 1293 Bellevue/Geneva, Switzerland.

When you register your information in the online Candidate Area, you will be asked to agree and tick this Candidate Privacy Statement before submitting your details.

By clicking on the "consent" button in the e-recruitment application the candidate confirms that he/she has read and given their explicit consent to the processing of their Personal Information and to the disclosure of their Personal Information as described above.

LES DISPOSITIONS QUI SUIVENT VISANT AU RESPECT DE LA VIE PRIVEE ET DE LA CONFIDENTIALITE TRAITENT DE LA COLLECTE, DE L'EXPLOITATION ET DE LA DIFFUSION DES DONNEES PERSONNELLES PAR LA SOCIETE RICHEMONT INTERNATIONAL SA, CI-APRES DENOMMEE RICHEMONT.

Richemont International S.A. (en adelante, "RICHEMONT"), con oficina en 50, chemin de la Chenaie, CH - 1293 Bellevue/Ginebra, Suiza, como responsable del almacenamiento de datos, y el departamento de Recursos Humanos como responsable de tratamiento y controlador del procesamiento de la informacion personal que es objeto de la presente Declaracion en lo relativo a candidaturas de trabajo, procesaran informacion enviada a RICHEMONT tanto en papel como en formato electronico. Los candidatos pueden, voluntariamente o bajo solicitud, enviar su historial profesional u otra informacion personal tales como su nombre, direccion de correo electronico, direccion postal, numero de telefono, y otras informaciones concernientes a sus empleos anteriores (todo ello denominado en adelante "Informacion Personal").

En caso de que no se conceda el consentimiento al tratamiento o que la Informacion Personal este incompleta, RICHEMONT podra rechazar candidaturas, especialmente cuando falte Informacion Personal necesaria para evaluar a los candidatos durante el periodo de seleccion, asi como para contactar con los candidatos en relacion con su estado dentro del proceso de seleccion. Adicionalmente, si los candidatos se convirtiesen posteriormente en empleados del Grupo Richemont en cualquiera de sus filiales, se incorporara su Informacion Personal a un archivo que puede ser usado con el fin de administracion del personal (incluyendo nominas, pensiones, gestion del desarrollo profesional y otros asuntos vinculados a su relacion laboral)

La Informacion Personal se comparte entre los departamentos de RICHEMONT y las Marcas pertenecientes al Grupo por cuestiones operativas (por ejemplo, para evaluar la idoneidad de candidaturas). Tambien se puede comunicar la Informacion Personal a entidades externas con las que RICHEMONT tenga una obligacion de informar; y en caso de establecerse una relacion laboral, tambien con los departamentos encargados de gestionar los impuestos, anticipos, pensiones y relaciones con las autoridades locales, etc.

Adicionalmente, la Informacion Personal de los candidatos y empleados se puede enviar a las companias afiliadas al Grupo Richemont, tanto dentro como fuera de Suiza, toda vez que el Grupo Richemont esta presente en un gran numero de paises.

Con respecto al proceso que realiza RICHEMONT sobre la Informacion Personal de sus candidatos y empleados, se mantienen los derechos garantizados por la Ley Suiza de Proteccion de Datos (RS 235.1 Loi federale sur la protection des donnees (LPD) de 19 de junio de 1992) y sus modificaciones ulteriores, en particular el derecho a recibir confirmacion de la existencia o no de tal Informacion Personal en posesion de RICHEMONT, el modo en que se utiliza, el derecho a recibir comunicaciones de un modo comprensible acerca de su Informacion Personal y su origen. Los candidatos tambien pueden solicitar que se elimine su Informacion Personal si no van a ser empleados por RICHEMONT, asi como actualizar, modificar, o solicitar que su Informacion Personal se convierta en anonima.

Si los candidatos no quieren que RICHEMONT use su Informacion Personal para los propositos descritos en esta declaracion, no deben enviar su candidatura a RICHEMONT.

RICHEMONT utilizara los medios que esten a su alcance para que la Informacion Personal se mantenga segura, en particular velara por que el acceso a la Informacion Personal este protegido por claves en todo momento, y que el acceso a los perfiles de los candidatos sea realizado exclusivamente por personal autorizado por RICHEMONT.

Nuestros empleados son parte de una familia global que trabaja en un ambiente justo, abierto y de confianza. Es importante para nosotros asegurar que los empleados nuevos que se unen el Grupo tiene un fuerte compromiso con nuestros valores compartidos.

Nos reservamos el derecho de pedir a todos los candidatos en las etapas finales de la contratacion para someterse a un 'pre-employment screening'. Nuestro enfoque del 'pre-employment screening' se basa en los principios de transparencia, respeto mutuo y se lleva a cabo de acuerdo con todas las regulaciones aplicables.

En caso de que los candidatos tengan preguntas acerca del uso de su Informacion Personal, pueden contactar con RICHEMONT en group.humanresources©richemont.com.
